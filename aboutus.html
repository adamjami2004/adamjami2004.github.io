Who are we ?

The Rolland-Garros association (RGA) is a tennis association founded in 2004 that hosts private lessons in Ottawa for players from all levels (beginner to competitive) and all ages starting from 5, with experts of the sports who will help you reach your full potential



Why we do it ?
We strongly believe that every tennis player can reach their full potential with the right type of help. We adapt our trainings depending on your type of play and your objectives to make your tennis journey as good as possible


Our coaches

Our team of coaches is composed of 4 professional coaches who are considered as the best in the world. Each coach has a favorite surface and a specific speciality, however, thanks to their experience, they can adapt to every type of player and always make you progress independently of your style or level



Our services

The RGA offers private tennis lessons. We offer either individual or group session (maximum of 3 people).

Our pricing politic is the following one:

Price per person:

-Half an hour session: 75$ + tax

-One hour session: 100$ + tax

If you are looking for package sessions, you can send us an email at RGA@uottawa.ca or call us at +1(613)219-8782

The training sessions are adaptive. That means that the coaches can help you on any specific aspect of the game that you want to improve. At the moment of the booking, you can specify if you have something you want to do. This will help your coach prepare your session with special exercises and introduction.
