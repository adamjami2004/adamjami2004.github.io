Holger Rune

Biography
Age: 45
Speciality: Serve and return
Favorite surface: Grass
Born in Denmark, Holger Rune is an ex-professionnal tennis player. During his carreer, he managed to win 4 Grand Slams and 7 ATP 1000 Titles. Due to several knee injuries, he retired in 2001 after 12 year on the professional circuit. Since then, he became the personnal coach of multiples players like Casper Ruud, Jose Cerundolo, and Andre Rublev. We managed to make them win 28 titles on total.
Since 2017, he's coaching his son, Holger Rune Jr, on the ATP circuit. During the last 6 years, Rune Jr has won The tournament of Roland-Garros once, the Miami Open twice and the Barcelona Open 4 times
During his carreer, Holger Senior has build a strong reputation around his serve. He's now considered as one of the best servers in the history
